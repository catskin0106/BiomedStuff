#### **Heart Failure**
**Definition of Heart Failure**
- Inability of Heart to pump blood effectively for body needs

**Symptoms of Heart Failure**
- ↓ Cardiac Muscle Contractility
- ↑ / ↓ Heart Rate (Arrhythmias)
