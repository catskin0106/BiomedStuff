- Subjective Emotional Disorder

	- Physical Symptoms: Palpitations, Tremor, Sweating
- Acute / Chronic

**Chronic Anxiety**
- <abbr Title="Generalized Anxiety Disorder">GAD</abbr>
- Society Anxiety Disorder
- PTSD & OCD
