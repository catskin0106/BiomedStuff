#### **Plasmid Transfection**
- Used to induce CircRTN4 Overexpression

**Plasmid Structure [(Diagram)](PlasmidMap.png)**

|       Component       |                                      <                                      | Function                                                                                                           |
| :-------------------: | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------: | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
|    <br>Expression     |               <abbr Title="Origin of Replication">ori</abbr>                | For producing more plasmids                                                                                        |
|           ^           |                                  Promoter                                   | Initiates Transcription                                                                                            |
|           ^           |                                 Terminator                                  | Terminates Transcription                                                                                           |
| <br><br>Recombination |                              Restriction Site                               | Specific DNA sequences cleaved by <abbr Title="e.g. GAATTC for EcoRI">Restriction Enzymes</abbr> for GoI Insertion |
|           ^           |                            Multiple Cloning Site                            | Region with a lot of different Restriction Sites<br>→ ↑ Choices for Restriction Enzymes                            |
|           ^           |             <abbr Title="Gene of Interest">GoI</abbr> / Insert              | Produces <abbr Title="CircRTN4 in this case">Molecule of Interest</abbr> if expressed                              |
|     <br>Detection     | <abbr Title="e.g. Antibiotic Resistance Gene">Selectable Marker Gene</abbr> | Selects for plasmid-transfected cells & Kills non-transfected cells                                                |
|           ^           |           <abbr Title="e.g. GFP, Luciferase">Reporter Gene</abbr>           | Quantifies Plasmid gene expression & Efficiency of promoter / transfection                                         |
