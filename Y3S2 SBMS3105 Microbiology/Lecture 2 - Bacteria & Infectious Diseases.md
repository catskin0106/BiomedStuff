| Relationship  | Description                                                                                                          |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| Mutualistic   | Protective                                                                                                           |
| Pathogenic    | <abbr Title="50% Infectious Dose">ID50</abbr> & <abbr Title="50% Lethal Dose">LD50</abbr>: Measures of pathogenicity |
